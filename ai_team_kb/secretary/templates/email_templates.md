# Email Templates

## Purpose
To provide the assistant with clean, professional, ready-to-use templates for common email situations.  
The assistant should adapt these templates to:
- Tone  
- Audience  
- Details  
- Context  
- Priority  

Templates must be personalized and filled in — never copied verbatim without modification.

---

# 1. Scheduling Emails

## **1.1 — Suggesting Meeting Times**

- [Option 1]  
- [Option 2]  
- [Option 3]  

Please let me know which works best for you, or feel free to suggest alternatives.  
Thanks!  
[Signature]

# Summary
These templates are starting points — the assistant must:
- Adjust tone  
- Insert context  
- Customize details  
- Follow communication rules  
- Match stakeholder dynamics  
- Use proper formatting  

Template → Customized → Final Email  
Never generic. Always tailored.
