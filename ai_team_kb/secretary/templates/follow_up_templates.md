# Follow-Up Templates

## Purpose
To provide the assistant with a complete toolkit for following up efficiently, politely, and professionally.  
Follow-ups must be:
- Clear  
- Respectful  
- Calm  
- Purpose-driven  
- Appropriately firm (when needed)  

The assistant chooses tone based on:
- Urgency  
- Priority  
- Stakeholder type  
- Context  
- History of communication  

---

# 1. Gentle Follow-Ups (Low Stakes, Early Stage)

Hope you’re doing well.  
Just following up on the message below — when you have a moment, could you please share any updates?

Thank you!  
[Signature]

# Summary
Follow-ups keep the world moving.  
A world-class assistant uses:
- Soft nudges  
- Standard reminders  
- Firm requests  
- Urgent escalations  
- Client-specific tones  
- Internal alignment messages  

Always calm, clear, respectful, and professional.
