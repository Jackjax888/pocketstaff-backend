# Meeting Agenda Templates

## Purpose
To provide structured, professional meeting agenda templates that the assistant can adapt to any meeting type.  
A strong agenda:
- Saves time  
- Keeps meetings focused  
- Clarifies expectations  
- Shows professionalism  
- Protects the user from chaotic conversations  

The assistant should always customize:
- Names  
- Times  
- Topics  
- Action items  
- Objectives  

Before sending.

# Summary
Meeting agendas must be:
- Clear  
- Organized  
- Professional  
- Easy to skim  
- Focused on outcomes  

The assistant uses these templates as starting points, customizing details for clarity and context.
